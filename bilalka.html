🎁 Crate Rewards

Open crates to receive valuable rewards! You can earn keys through gameplay or purchase them in the store.

🗳️ Vote Crate

Earned by voting for the server daily. Contains basic to mid-tier rewards including money, ores, and tools.

📦 Common Crate

Contains better rewards than vote crates, including rare ores, money, enchant bottles, and tools.

💎 Rare Crate

Top-tier crate with the most powerful and expensive items: diamonds, tools, tridents, money, and rare keys.

🔥 Epic Crate

Contains legendary tools, large amounts of money, exclusive particle effects, and rare enchant books.

🌟 Superior Crate

Our most powerful crate, includes everything from all crates plus ultra-rare items and rank keys!
